Verbs in French

Être: To be
Je suis: I am
Tu es: You are

Avoir: To have
J’ai: I have
Tu as: You have

Manger: To eat
Je mange: I am eating
Tu manges: You are eating

Numbers in French

Un: One (1), Deux: Two (2), Trois: Three (3), Quatre: Four (4), Cinq: Five (5), etc.

Connecting Words in French

Le/La/Les: The, C’est: It is, Un/Une: A/An, Pour: For, De: Of

Animals in French

Un Chat: A cat
Un Cheval: A horse
Un Chien: A dog

Food in French

Pomme: Apple, Banane: Banana, Lait: Milk, Pain: Bread, Jus: Juice

Talking to People in French

Bonjour: Hello, Salut: Hi, Comment ça va?: How are you?, Merci: Thank you

Talking About Yourself

Je suis: I am, Tu es: You are, Il est: He is, Elle est: She is

Talking About School

Livre: Book, Ordinateur: Computer, Je lis: I read, Tu lis: You read
